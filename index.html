So what is Ignite@CU?

The third annual Ignite@CU is an intercollegiate entrepreneurship competition taking place on February 11th and 12th at Columbia University. Over the course of 36 hours, participants will build a business from scratch before pitching it in front of experienced investors. Ignite is also a great opportunity for the nation's top collegiate entrepreneurial talent to come together and exchange ideas.

Who can attend?

Anyone currently enrolled in an undergraduate institution! If your college's entrepreneurship club isn't participating, we'd still love to have you! Better yet, point us to the right people to get your club involved!

Can you arrange overnight accomodations?

Yes! The conference will have ample room for people to sleep when they are not building their business. All youâ€™ll need is a sleeping bag, toiletries, and a change of clothes!

Are there prizes?

No competition would be complete without awards! Winners will have the opportunity to meet one-on-one with venture capitalists to either further develop their idea or obtain advice about other ventures they may have.

Is technical experience required?

Absolutely not! Although students may choose to build their product if they bring the materials, ideas will be primarily judged on their innovation and market viability, not their current functionality.
